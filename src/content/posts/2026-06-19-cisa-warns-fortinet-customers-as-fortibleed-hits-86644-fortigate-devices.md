The US Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) has issued a warning to Fortinet customers with FortiGate appliances, urging them to take immediate action to secure their devices against ongoing malicious activity. This campaign, codenamed FortiBleed, is believed to be the work of Russian-speaking threat actors and has already compromised a significant number of devices. 

As many as 86,644 FortiGate devices have been hit by FortiBleed, leaving them vulnerable to further exploitation. The fact that these devices are internet-accessible makes them an attractive target for threat actors. 

CISA's warning serves as a reminder for Fortinet customers to prioritize the security of their FortiGate appliances and take necessary steps to protect against potential attacks.
