Dutch law enforcement authorities, in collaboration with counterparts from Canada, Germany, and the US, have successfully disrupted the malicious infrastructure associated with SocGholish. This operation, known as Operation Endgame, has resulted in the cleanup of 14,971 infected WordPress websites. By taking down these servers, authorities have effectively severed cybercriminals' access to compromised computer systems.

Maikel Rollman of the Netherlands National High Tech Crime Unit emphasized the impact of this operation, stating that it deprives cybercriminals of control over infected systems. This significant disruption is expected to hinder the SocGholish malware campaign, which had been leveraging infected websites to spread further. 

The takedown of SocGholish infrastructure marks a notable victory for international law enforcement cooperation in the fight against cybercrime, protecting countless users from potential threats.
